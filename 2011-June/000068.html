> Am I supposed to use something else rather then COM*..? 

Yes, on GNU/Linux you usually get /dev/ttyUSB0 or something similar.

You can run "dmesg" from a terminal right after you have plugged in the
cable, and you should read the last lines of the output to see which
virtual port was created.

We have some plans to automatically detect ports at runtime, but it's
still in the TODO list.
