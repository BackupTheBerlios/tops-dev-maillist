Hi,
today I've been able to play a bit with a Nikon NPL-352 total station.

At first I was thinking about an easy thing because I already had some
sample data from a Nikon NPL-350 which is almost exactly the same model.
But when I got the data downloaded and tried to convert them to DXF for
visualization, it was clear that something was wrong.

Actually, the Nikon RAW data format brings distance/angle measurements
and not XYZ values! So, it's impossible to convert them directly, at
least with our current code base.

I'm trying to understand whether it's possible to download data already
in XYZ format. My friend who uses the total station says it's not, but
she'll investigate too.

More news coming soon...
