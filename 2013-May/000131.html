My name is Filip Klosowski and I'm from Poland.
I would be very glad if I could be helpful in any way.

Now, I'd like to provide some useful, at this point, general RW5 info 
just to consider.
Since Carslon SurvCE is rather external data collector multifunctional 
software and only rarely total station onboard,it is important from the 
start to be aware of:
- sometimes, single RW5 job file may comprise mixed data, collected by 
TS, GPS and even Level
- what kind of data conversion would be useful to end user or which 
records are importatnt for TOPS
- there are two or even three variants of RW5 file, Carlson's one, 
so-called TDS RW5 [created by Tripod Data Systems, a company 
subsequently incorporated to Trimble] and possibly Microsurvey's one - 
it is believed to be
identical as TDS RW5 but some converters, like Leica X-change, list all 
three variants.
http://www.microsurvey.com/support/fieldgenius/documentation/TDSRawDataSpec.pdf
...so maybe just to write TOPS module to handle both types [or three].

Finally, I'd like to ask what method of parsing RW5 file, you think, is 
right?
In my testing converter I utilized pyparsing
https://pypi.python.org/pypi/pyparsing/2.0.0
